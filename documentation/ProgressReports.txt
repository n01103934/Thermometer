Progress reports are to be posted on the weeks that they are due and are to include:
•	A description of recent project activities with reference to the project schedule.
•	Current progress in meeting the objectives of the project as defined in the approved proposal.
•	Description of any problems/opportunities encountered and modifications being made to address them.
•	Financial updates on the use of project funds, along with a brief commentary where significant variances exist from the budget.
•	Links to repository copies of any published media coverage, promotional materials, reports, documents or publications arising from the project.
